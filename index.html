Hey there!

I'm Mads, if I'm not designing at my computer, you'll probably find me on the floor drawing or scrolling Pinterest for inspiration. For me, creating is more than just a hobby or a job. It's a part of who I am, and it always has been. To say I am obsessed is an understatement.

I am currently making my way through the design program at WWU and have already accomplished a handful of brand projects and website builds along the way. I've also dipped my toes in packaging and animation. It's nearly impossible for me to turn down an opportunity to grow in my creative abilities. Again, completely obsessed. Thanks for popping by and seeing what I'm about!
